# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard (Overview)
## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Car Sales Dashboard designed to analyze year-to-date (YTD) sales performance across multiple dimensions such as company, body style, color, and pricing trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify insights, trends, and opportunities in automotive sales data.


## 🎯 Objectives
* Track overall sales performance and growth
* Analyze car sales by brand, body style, and color
* Monitor pricing trends and average sales price
* Identify top-performing companies and sales drivers

---

## 📌 Key Metrics
* Total Sales: $672M
* YTD Sales: $371M
* Total Cars Sold: 24K
* YTD Cars Sold: 13K
* Average Price: $28K

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
* Sales Trend Analysis: Weekly YTD sales trends visualization
* Sales by Color: Distribution of sales across different car colors
* Sales by Body Style: Comparison across SUV, Hatchback, Sedan, etc.
* Top 10 Companies: Ranking based on total sales
* Company-Level Insights:
* YTD Sales
* Cars Sold
* Growth %
* Average Price



---

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* Strong Growth: Sales reached $672M with +23.6% growth, driven mainly by higher volumes, not pricing.
* Volume-Led Performance: 24K cars sold (+24.6%), showing strong demand.
* Top Brands: Chevrolet, Ford, and Dodge lead in both revenue and units sold.
* SUV Dominance: SUVs are the top-selling segment, indicating high customer preference.
* Color Trends: White and black cars account for the majority of sales.
* Pricing Pressure: Average price slightly declined (-0.8%), hinting at discounts or shift to lower-priced models.
* Seasonal Trends: Weekly sales fluctuate, suggesting impact of promotions or seasonality.
